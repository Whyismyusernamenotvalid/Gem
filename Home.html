A Short Message

Hello Gem, I know that this words is something I say everyday, but I love you, I adore you, I want you. I am grateful for everything, especially the little things you do. They may seems small or ordinary to you, but to me they mean so much. Those actions are enough to get me to smile even if everything seems to fall apart. I do hope that you enjoy being with me, and I hope that you achieve everything that you deserve, because to me, you deserve everything.

I love you.

A Princess Crowned with Gems

Eyes embedded with magic,
Enough to hold one captive.
Hair as long as her love,
For the peasant who wears it like a glove.

Treasured by many, loved by one,
Oh, how sorrowful will it be if you were gone?
I thank the gem from the princess's crown
For the soothing words that healed my frown.

No one cares for what a peasant say,
Yet you've heard every story i've preached every day.
Gratitude cannot potray,
How your greatness made me stay.
Insolence is the crown for those who have disrespected,
Someone this humble peasant has protected.
